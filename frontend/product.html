Description HTC Sensation XE

The HTC Sensation XE is a smartphone that is crafted with style and finesse. This touchscreen phone runs on Android v2.3.4 (Gingerbread) operating system and has a 1.5 GHz dual core processor integrated in to it. This HTC smartphone has a preloaded RAM size of 768 MB. HTC Sense, which is the UI, enables your phone with applications like instant capture camera and active lock screen that makes your mobile experience exciting.

A powerful 8 megapixel camera on the HTC Sensation, along with auto-focus and a powerful dual LED, allows you to shoot pictures at a maximum resolution of 3264 x 2448 pixels. Geo tagging is supported on the phone.

Touch-focus, image stabilization, face detection and instant capture on this HTC handset make photography easy and fun. A secondary camera on the phone can be used to make video calls. You can record Full HD 1080p videos at 30 fps with stereo sound recording feature. The HTC Sensation XE handset is a sleek elegantly built phone.
